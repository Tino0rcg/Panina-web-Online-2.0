# **App Name**: TecnoConecta

## Core Features:

- Professional Homepage: A welcoming landing page introducing the consulting company, its mission, and key offerings, with a clear call to action.
- Detailed Services Showcase: Comprehensive pages detailing each IT engineering consulting service, including benefits, methodology, and typical project outcomes.
- About Us & Team: An 'About Us' section presenting the company's values, vision, and a professional introduction to the team members.
- AI-Powered Service Matcher Tool: An interactive tool allowing potential clients to input their business challenges or needs, and receive tailored recommendations for the company's relevant consulting services.
- Client Testimonials: A dedicated section to display client feedback and success stories, building trust and credibility.
- Contact and Inquiry Form: An easy-to-use contact form for prospective clients to submit inquiries and schedule consultations, with direct communication channels.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A sophisticated, professional blue (#6699FF) to convey trust and innovation against dark backgrounds.
- Background color: A very dark, subtle blue-grey (#15181C) for a sleek, modern, and professional dark-themed interface.
- Accent color: A vibrant cyan (#2EC4DF) to highlight interactive elements, calls-to-action, and key information, providing a striking contrast.
- Headlines: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, tech-savvy, and professional feel.
- Body text: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for excellent legibility and a clean, objective presentation of information.
- Use modern, minimalist line icons that subtly represent technological concepts, data, and business solutions.
- Employ a clean, structured, and modular layout with ample whitespace to ensure readability and focus on content, adapting seamlessly across all device sizes.
- Implement subtle and elegant hover animations for buttons and links, along with smooth transitions for section reveals and navigation, to enhance user experience without distraction.
